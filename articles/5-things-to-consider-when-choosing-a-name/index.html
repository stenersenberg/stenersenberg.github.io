5 things to consider when choosing a name

Choosing a name is an exciting, but also a challenging task awaiting expectant parents. Here are some important aspects to consider when you are looking for the perfect baby name.

Uniqueness vs. popularity

Is it important to you that the child's name is not widely used? Some parents prefer rare and unique names to give the child a special identity, while others prefer more familiar names that are easily accepted and recognized in society.

Elara
A unique and rare name.
Caspian
A rare, but catchy name.
Emma
A popular and well-known name. Emma has a timeless charm and has been popular in many countries for several years.
Lucas
A name that is also popular in many countries. Lucas is a classic that combines modern sound with historical depth.

Daring the unusual

Do you dare to choose a name that attracts attention or is entirely unique? Choosing an unusual name can be a way to express individuality, but it's also important to consider how the name will be received by others, and how it will affect the child in the future.

Zephyr
A unique and striking name. Zephyr means a gentle breeze, and gives a sense of nature and lightness.
Andromeda
A name that stands out and is very unique. Andromeda is also the name of a galaxy.

Family and surname combination

Think about how the chosen first name will sound with the surname. Is it important to you that the name should harmonize with family names? This can also include considering how the name fits with the names of any siblings.

Internationality

In an increasingly globalized world, it might be useful to consider if the child's name works well internationally. This can be especially relevant for families with an international background, or who plan to live or travel a lot abroad.

Mia
A short and internationally recognized name. Mia is easy to pronounce in several languages and carries a sweet and timeless appeal.
Leo
A simple, but strong international name. Leo, meaning 'lion', is known and respected in many cultures and languages.

Personal values and meanings

If there are any values or concepts that are particularly important to you, you can reflect this in the choice of name. This can include names that symbolize strength, nature, art, etc.

Iris
A name that symbolizes rainbow and beauty. Iris is also known in Greek mythology as the goddess of the rainbow, adding a mythological touch.
Mateo
Mateo means 'gift of God'. It conveys a sense of being blessed and cherished

Listening to intuition

Finally, do not underestimate the power of your own intuition. Sometimes a name just 'feels right'. Trust your gut feeling and good luck!
